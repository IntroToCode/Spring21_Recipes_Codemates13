# Ground Beef Spaghetti

## Servings: 4 people

## Time to prepare: 60 minutes

## Ingredients: cloves garlic, green bell pepper, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, black pepper, spaghetti


## Equipment needed:a pot, a pan


## Steps for preparation:
1. combine ground beef, onion garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. frain grease.
2. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato suace, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occassionally.
3. Mix the sauce with the cooked spaghetti. 



### Notes:
Make sure to add salt to water to cook spaghetti.
