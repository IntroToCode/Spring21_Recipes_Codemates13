# French Toasts üçû

## Servings: 4

## Time to prepare: 30 minutes (20 minutes preparation / 10 minutes cooking)

## Ingredients:
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 8 slices white bread

Opt: Syrup!

## Equipment needed:
- bowl
- skillet
- container / shallow plate

## Steps for preparation:
1. In a bowl, combine cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar - set asidee
2. melt butter in a skillet (medium heat). Whisk together (1) mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla and pour into a shallow container
3. Fry slices until golden brown, then flip to cook the other side
4. Optional: Serve with syrup!
